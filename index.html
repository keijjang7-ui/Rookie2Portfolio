Meet Rookie2

A service robot shaped for real indoor environments, balancing technology, usability, and a familiar presence.

A familiar presence in everyday spaces.

Quietly becoming part of everyday life.

Built for Smart Buildings

Bringing together cloud intelligence, autonomous mobility, and building systems into a connected service layer for robotic delivery.

Connected through 5G and NAVER Cloud, Rookie2 continuously shares intelligence across a connected robotic ecosystem.

A real-time digital understanding of indoor spaces enables Rookie2 to navigate with confidence.

Every robot works as part of one connected system, sharing information across the building in real time.

Seamlessly connect NAVER WORKS and NAVER Order for a better service experience.

Stay informed with real-time robot updates and service notifications.

Request service changes easily and follow every step of the process.

Access your service history and current service information anytime.

From packages and drinks to lunchboxes, Rookie2 keeps everyday deliveries moving throughout modern buildings.

Built on NAVER’s second headquarters, the world’s first robot-friendly building, Rookie leverages state-of-the-art technologies and seamlessly integrates with next-generation spatial infrastructure.

Natural gaze interaction enables intuitive and comfortable communication.

Human-friendly navigation ensures smooth, safe, and non-threatening movement.

Seamlessly access elevators and ROBOPORT™ for continuous autonomous operation.
